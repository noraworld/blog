This blog is a place for the author to keep personal thoughts and records.<br>
It is not a place for debates or discussions.

In the comment section, we only accept agreement, support, and positive feedback about the posts.<br>
Please do not post criticism, disagreement, rebuttals, or comments aiming to change the author's views.

Comments may be deleted at the administrator's discretion, regardless of content.<br>
Additionally, accounts repeatedly posting comments that don't follow the guidelines may be blocked without notice.

All decisions are made at the discretion of the administrator, and no individual explanations will be provided.

We ask for your understanding in keeping this space as a peaceful place of record.
