Daydreams taste like sweet honey. They fill a huge, bottomless jar, which you can never finish even if you spend your whole life trying.

If you lose yourself in licking it, you might fall into the jar and get covered in sticky honey.

Once you're stuck, it's not easy to get out. The trap of sweet honey will slowly and surely lure you into a bottomless honey swamp...

Do not resist. The more you struggle, the more the sweet honey trap will firmly catch your feet. Quietly observe reality and notice the taste of honey.
