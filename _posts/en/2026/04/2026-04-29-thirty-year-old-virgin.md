## I Resumed Using Dating Apps After a Long Time...
Just a few days ago (on the 25th), I opened a dating app for the first time in a while. As I wrote "for the first time in a while," it’s not my first time using a dating app. I created an account in 2019 and was initially using it actively, but I didn’t find any good matches and gradually stopped using it.

Since then, I occasionally feel like trying again, but things don’t go well, so I stop quickly. Since it's usually a paid service for men, I can't just keep it indefinitely. Also, as I try more times, I keep thinking it won’t go well again, so even if I decide to become a paid member, I usually opt for a one-month plan.

I often give up and stop opening the app before my paid membership expires, and there are long gaps between restarts. So, since I started in 2019, I have spent far more time not using the app.

I've tried the four well-known apps since the beginning (Omiai, Pairs, with, Tapple). At first, I paid for multiple apps to run them simultaneously, but I know now that spending money like that is wasteful. So, I become a paid member only when I match with several people on the app with good responses (like counts and match rates) after free messaging is unavailable.

Which app has good responses changes from time to time, but this time I matched with 3-4 people on Omiai (there was no response from others), so I became a paid member of Omiai for just one month.

### No Buzz, No Wing...
As expected, there aren’t any prospects of things going well this time either. The first person I matched with liked me first and greeted me casually, but after exchanging a few messages, I saw a reply from them, but by the time I opened the app, their account was already deactivated (blocked?).

The second person I matched with didn’t respond to my greeting, and like the first person, their account became deactivated. The second person also liked me first, but it was probably a mistaken tap or something. Once you like someone, you can't undo it, which often leads to false hopes.

The third and subsequent matches were foreigners who had to return to their home countries soon or took forever to reply, so I already felt it would probably not lead to a connection.

### No Thrill...
Honestly, it’s not even about whether it goes well or not—I just don’t get butterflies... I know it's really rude to say, but somehow, everyone looks the same to me. The same kind of photos, the same type of self-introduction texts, the same profiles... Swiping through profile after profile, it all feels like a monotonous task that I’m just endlessly repeating.

I know. I opened the app looking for a connection, after all. But once I opened it, all I saw was the endless repetition of the same sights... I looked for it myself, yet I find no interest at all... What is this...?
