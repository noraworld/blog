## A Couple's Conversation
**Woman:** “Hey, I heard there's going to be a big fireworks festival nearby on this day! There will be lots of food stands, too. Sounds fun! Are you free that day?”

**Man:** “Oh, nice. It's been a while since I watched fireworks, and I want to see you in a yukata. Let me check my schedule real quick.”

The man checks if he's free.

**Man:** “Ah, I have work that day... But no problem, I'll take a day off using my paid leave.”

**Woman:** “Oh no, it's fine. There are other small fireworks festivals. You don't have to push yourself for me! Let's go together another time when you're free.”

**Man:** “No, I want to go to that fireworks festival with you on that day. I'm doing it because I want to. Any complaints? Haha.”

**Woman:** “Nope. No complaints at all. Let's have fun that day.”



## A Married Couple's Conversation
**Woman:** “Hey, thank you for always being thoughtful. Thank you for being kind.”

**Man:** “You don't have to thank me. I do it because I want to.”

**Woman:** “Even so, I really appreciate it. Thank you for always saying ‘I love you.’”

**Man:** “I love you because I want to. Is there any need for another reason?”

**Woman:** “You're right. I love you too.”

**Man:** “No matter what happens, I'm on your side.”



## What Love Is
Love is something given unconditionally. If you attach conditions, it's not love.

Think about it. If you're only attracted to someone because they're in shape, what happens if they gain weight? If you only find someone cool because they can make money, they won't seem cool if they lose their job. If you only like someone because they're kind, you wouldn't like them if they stopped being kind.

That's just pretty talk. Such ideal relationships where everything is perfect don't exist in the real world. I used to think so, too.

But now that I'm free from all attachments, I've realized it does exist. Of course, it’s not easy. There will be hurdles to overcome.

When you realize that everything in this world works together, you see that humans are born with love. That love gets hidden by all sorts of attachments throughout life.

Love is like a lotus flower. It blooms beautifully no matter how muddy the water is. Even if everything around it is dirty, the lotus remains pure.

The beauty of the lotus might get buried in mud sometimes. That's what hate is. When a loving couple starts hating each other, it's not that the lotus is tainted; it's just covered in mud.

Love is unconditional. It's only love when it's unconditional.



## Don't Get Too Full of Yourself
“I'm taking a day off work because I want to watch fireworks with you. **Don't get too full of yourself thinking I did it for you.**”

“I don't say 'I love you' because I want you to think well of me or to be thanked. It's not that I want you to love me back. I love you because I want to. **It's fine to be full of yourself for a bit, but don't overdo it.**”



## Fate
I hope to meet a partner with whom I can build such a happy relationship. But even if I don't, "Oh well."
