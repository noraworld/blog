Hello, I'm Nora. It's been a while since I wrote something like a poem, so I'm a little nervous.

## Why I Started Using note

I was doing some research, and one of the articles on note came up. It was just a small reason, but I wanted to secure my ID.

I already knew about note and thought its design was really stylish back then.

However, I'm a bit peculiar and believe that the best platform for writing is one you create yourself. That's why I publish my blog on a server I manage, with my own domain.

Note might not be exactly a blog, but in the context of writing, I personally see them as the same. Since I already have my own blog, I don't see the need to switch to note now.

But when I see that beautiful design, I can't help but be impressed. I have a tendency to want to try out stylish and pretty things, so even if I don't intend to use it, I feel like creating an account on such a wonderful platform.

Also, I really care about identity, to an extreme degree. I'm very concerned about whether I can get my ID on web services and the like. If I can't get my ID, I lose interest in using that service. That's how much I obsess over IDs.

When I looked at an article on note after a long time, I casually checked if I could get my ID and it looked possible, so I decided to create an account before someone else did. This is how, without intending to use it, I often create accounts just because the design is stylish or because I'd hate it if my ID was already taken.

I already have my own blog, so I might not write anything on note in the future. However, if the desire to express my thoughts in this stylish place arises, I might publish something again. For now, I created an account to secure my ID and thought I'd write one article for the occasion.

## Miscellaneous: My Obsession with Domains

Speaking of which, when I first knew about note, I think the domain was [note.mu](https://note.mu), but it seems to have changed to [note.com](https://note.com) without me noticing. I didn't know.

I've also spent about 300,000 yen in the past to buy the domain [noraworld.com](https://noraworld.com), so I completely understand the fixation on a domain name. It's the same as the obsession with an ID.

## Ending

Earlier, I mentioned that since I have a blog, I might not write on note, but actually I've been neglecting my blog for more than a year now... I was also keeping a diary besides my blog, but I stopped that around the end of last year, and haven't picked it up since. But writing like this after so long, I feel again that writing is indeed fun. I want to thank note just for helping me realize that again.

This might be the first and last article, but if I change my mind and write here again, I look forward to it.
